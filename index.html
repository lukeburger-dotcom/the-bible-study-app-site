Common questions

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?
Subscriptions are billed by Apple: iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions. Restore Purchases in the app recovers an existing subscription on a new device.

Is the Bible text real or AI-generated?
Displayed Scripture comes from real public-domain translations bundled in the app (BSB, KJV, WEB, ASV, YLT, Darby). The commentary is AI-generated and sourced — and the app invites you to check every quoted passage against the bundled text with one tap. For serious study, always verify against an authoritative edition.

Why aren't NASB, NIV, or ESV available?
Those translations require licenses, which we're pursuing. Version 1 ships with trustworthy public-domain translations; licensed ones arrive as agreements land.

Do I need an account?
No. There are no accounts. Your history stays on your device. See our Privacy Policy.
